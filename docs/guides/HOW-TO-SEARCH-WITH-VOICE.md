- ‚úÖ Processed in real-time

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### "The microphone icon is grayed out / disabled"

**Check:**
1. **Are you logged in?** Voice search requires an account. Sign in first.
2. **Is your account approved?** New accounts need admin approval (usually within 24 hours). Check your email for an approval notification.
3. **Have you used all your daily searches?** Each user gets 10/day. Check the quota badge ‚Äî if it says "0 remaining", try again tomorrow or use manual text search.

### "Voice search isn't working"

**Check:**
1. Microphone permission is allowed
2. Browser supports voice (Chrome, Safari, Edge work best)
3. Internet connection is stable
4. Microphone is not muted

**Still having issues?**
- Try manual search (type instead)
- Contact support
- Check system audio settings

### "It didn't understand me"

**Try:**
- Speaking more slowly
- Rephrasing your search
- Being more specific
- Reducing background noise

### "I want to switch back to typing"

Just use the regular search box! Voice is optional, always available.

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Do I need an account to use voice search?**
A: Yes. Voice search requires a logged-in, approved account. Manual text search is available to everyone.

**Q: How many voice searches can I do per day?**
A: Regular users get 10 voice searches per day, resetting at midnight. A badge near the search bar shows your remaining count.

**Q: What happens when I reach my daily limit?**
A: The microphone button becomes disabled. You can still use manual text search (unlimited) or wait until midnight when the counter resets.

**Q: Is my voice recorded?**
A: No, voice is processed in real-time and not stored.

**Q: Can I use voice search on mobile?**
A: Yes! Works great on iOS and Android.

**Q: What languages are supported?**
A: Currently English only. More languages coming soon!

**Q: Do I have to use voice search?**
A: No, it's completely optional. Manual search works just as well and has no daily limit.

**Q: Can voice search handle complex requests?**
A: Yes! Try: "Find 2-bedroom Hilton properties in Orlando with pool under $200/night near Disney"
